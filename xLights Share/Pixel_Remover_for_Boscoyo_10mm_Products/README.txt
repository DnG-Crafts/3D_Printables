# Summary

After pushing over 150 pixels in a Boscoyo snowflake, I realized I made a mistake on the pixel order and they all needed to be removed. This design removes pixels from Boscoyo 10mm thick coro products. Place over the pixel and push until flush with the front of the coro. The pixels will be loosely attached and will come out with a gentle pull from the backside.
